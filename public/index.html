阅读虾能做什么

What Read-again Does

重点不是重新概括一本书，而是帮你回收、理解、组织你曾经认真标记过的内容。

The point is not to summarize a whole book again. It is to recover, explain, and organize what you already cared enough to mark.

01

只提取你的笔记

Extracts only your notes

阅读虾只读取你高亮、下划线和批注的部分，不处理整本书，避免把注意力稀释在无关内容里。

Read-again processes highlights, underlines, and annotations only. It ignores the rest of the book so the result stays personal and relevant.

02

生成复习型笔记

Builds review-oriented notes

输出不是泛泛的摘要，而是一份围绕“你之前关注了什么”的复习资料，适合二次学习和快速回顾。

The output is not a generic summary. It is a review note centered on what you paid attention to before, ready for rereading and recall.

03

自动同步到飞书

Saves directly to Feishu

生成后的笔记会自动保存到飞书「AI 读书笔记」文件夹里，方便归档、检索、分享和长期沉淀。

Generated notes are stored automatically in the Feishu folder “AI Reading Notes” for archive, search, sharing, and long-term reuse.

为什么叫“阅读虾”

Why the name “Read-again”

“再读一遍”对应 Read-again；虾虽小但灵活，能快速啃下难读的硬骨头。这是一个偏轻巧、偏实用、但很贴学习过程的工具命名。

The name points to rereading, not replacing reading. The shrimp metaphor suggests something small, nimble, and unexpectedly capable of helping with hard texts.

为什么这不是摘要工具？

Why is this not just another summarizer?

因为输入的核心不是整本书，而是你留下的阅读痕迹。阅读虾的任务是重组你的关注点，而不是代替你的阅读过程。

Because the core input is not the whole book. It is your reading trace. Read-again reorganizes what you cared about instead of pretending to replace the reading process.

适合难书吗？

Is it good for difficult books?

很适合。哲学、社会学、技术理论这类难书，往往需要回头看重点和概念关系，阅读虾在这里价值很高。

Yes. It is especially useful for philosophy, sociology, theory, and technical texts where revisiting highlights and conceptual links matters.

为什么要同步飞书？

Why save to Feishu?

因为一份真正会被复习的笔记，必须先能被找到。自动保存让你的复习资料持续可检索、可积累、可分享。

Because notes only matter if you can find them later. Automatic saving makes the review archive searchable, accumulative, and shareable.
