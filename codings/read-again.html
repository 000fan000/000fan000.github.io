把旧 PDF 里的高亮，整理成可复习的笔记。

阅读虾是一个轻量的阅读工具。你把自己已经读过、做过标注的 PDF 发给它，它会提取你的高亮和批注，整理成一份更适合回看的复习笔记。

Turn your old PDF highlights into review notes.

Read-again is a small reading tool. You send it a PDF you already read and annotated, and it turns your highlights and comments into a cleaner note for review.

怎么用 示例 How it works Example

它做什么

What It Does

  • 只读取 PDF 里你高亮、批注过的部分。
  • 把这些片段整理成一份便于复习的笔记。
  • 把结果保存到飞书的读书笔记文件夹。
  • Reads only the parts you highlighted or annotated in the PDF.
  • Groups those fragments into a note you can reread later.
  • Saves the result to your Feishu reading-notes folder.

怎么用

How It Works

  1. 打开安装了阅读虾的聊天工具。
  2. 发送一份你已经读过并做过标注的 PDF。
  3. 阅读虾提取高亮和批注，生成复习笔记。
  4. 最终笔记会保存到飞书，方便之后回看。
  1. Open the chat tool where Read-again is installed.
  2. Send a PDF you have already read and marked up.
  3. Read-again extracts your highlights and builds a review note.
  4. The final note is saved to Feishu for later reading.

为什么做它

Why This Tool

重点不是再把整本书总结一遍，而是回到你自己的阅读痕迹：你标了什么、你当时觉得什么重要、你之后还想回看的是什么。

The point is not to summarize a whole book again. The point is to return to your own reading trace: what you marked, what you thought mattered, and what you may want to revisit.
